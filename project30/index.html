About Our Work

Welcome to VocaSmart, where innovation meets simplicity. Our cutting-edge voice recognition system leverages advanced technologies like MFCC and CNN, powered by the ESP32 microcontroller, to deliver secure, efficient, and user-friendly solutions for home automation and IoT applications. Whether you're looking to control your appliances seamlessly, enhance security with offline voice processing, or explore the future of smart systems, VocaSmart provides an intuitive and reliable platform. Designed for privacy and performance, our system works offline, ensuring your data stays secure while offering real-time functionality. Discover the power of voice technology with 'VocaSmart'—the smarter way to connect with your world.
